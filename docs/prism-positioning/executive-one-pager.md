# PRISM: Enterprise Modernization Intelligence Platform

## Executive One-Pager

---

### The Challenge

State and federal central IT organizations are modernizing toward federated data environments, but this creates a critical visibility gap. Financial activity, cloud consumption, modernization progress, and delivery risk are dispersed across agencies, platforms, and vendors, each operating on different timelines and reporting cadences.

The result: **Budget surprises, delayed chargebacks, and reactive decision-making.**

By the time issues surface in formal reporting cycles, opportunities to reallocate funds or course-correct have already passed.

---

### The Solution: PRISM

PRISM is an enterprise intelligence platform built on Snowflake that transforms fragmented modernization signals into a unified, real-time decision surface for executive leadership.

**PRISM is not a dashboard.** It is an always-on intelligence system that elevates the highest-quality signals for executive action.

---

### Key Capabilities

| Capability | Executive Value |
|------------|-----------------|
| **Real-Time Financial Intelligence** | See spending and consumption as it happens, not months later |
| **Incurred-But-Not-Billed Exposure** | Anticipate chargebacks before invoices post |
| **Continuous Forecasting** | Rolling forecasts with confidence bands for proactive planning |
| **Anomaly Detection with Explanation** | Understand not just what deviated, but why and what to do |
| **Governed AI Narratives** | Decision-ready insights with full audit trail |
| **Hybrid Snowflake Intelligence** | In-context guidance plus full analytical power when needed |

---

### Business Outcomes

**Prevent Fiscal Disruption**
Identify budget risk 60-90 days earlier than traditional reporting cycles

**Eliminate Surprise Chargebacks**
Model incurred exposure continuously, enabling proactive vendor engagement

**Accelerate Decision Cycles**
Move from quarterly reconciliation to weekly intelligence-driven steering

**Demonstrate Modernization ROI**
Measurable, explainable outcomes that satisfy legislative and audit requirements

**Reduce Integrator Dependency**
Integrator-agnostic design ensures enterprise oversight independent of any single vendor
